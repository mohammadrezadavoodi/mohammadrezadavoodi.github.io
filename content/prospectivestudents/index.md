Our research is multi-disciplinary and prospective students may have strong backgrounds in all or some of the following research areas:

* Systems and control theory
* Complex systems
* Robotics
* Power systems

Please see Research to get familiar with our projects.

If you are interested in joining our group, please mention that in your application to the graduate program in EECE at University of Memphis. You can also send an email to [mdavoodi[AT]memphis[DOT]edu](mailto:mdavoodi@memphis.edu) with your CV, transcripts, and past research experience (preferably all in one pdf file).
