Mohammadreza Davoodi is a Research Scientist III at the University of Texas at Arlington Research Institute (UTARI), division of Automation & Intelligent Systems. He received the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Tarbiat Modares University (Iran) in 2008 and 2012, respectively. Before joining UTARI, he was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at both the University of Georgia and the Qatar University (Qatar), and a Visiting Researcher at the Concordia University (Canada).
